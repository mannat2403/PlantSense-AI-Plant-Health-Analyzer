Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I water my plants?

Most indoor plants need watering once a week. However, succulents and cacti require less frequent watering.

What type of light is best for plants?

Different plants have different light requirements. Some thrive in direct sunlight, while others need shade.

Why are my plant’s leaves turning yellow?

Yellow leaves can be caused by overwatering, underwatering, or nutrient deficiencies.

What is the best soil for indoor plants?

Indoor plants thrive in well-draining soil mixed with organic compost.
